Butternut Squash Ramen

This meal is provides a healthy alternative to conventional Ramen. You'll appreciate the simplicity of this recipe as well at its health benefits.

Photo Credit: Eating Well

Ingredients

  • 1 medium Buttnut squash
  • 1 half dozen eggs
  • 3 cups broth
  • 1 bundle Kale
  • 1 spiralizer

Cooking Instructions

  1. Set oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cut butternut squash into long thick chunks to run through spiralizer.
  3. Place spiralized product on a baking sheet coated in olive oil. Bake for 10 minutes.
  4. Heat up broth and kale in medium pot.
  5. When cooked add butternut squash noodles to soup broth and serve.
